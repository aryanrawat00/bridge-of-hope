About HopeBridge

A decade of turning empathy into action.

Founded in 2011 by a group of educators and social workers, HopeBridge has grown into a nationwide movement — reaching under-served communities with dignity, data and design.

◎

Our Mission

To create equal access to education, healthcare and opportunity for every child and family.

✧

Our Vision

A world where circumstance never limits a person's potential to thrive.

♥

Our Values

Empathy, integrity, transparency, courage and community-first design.

Our journey

  1. 2011

    Founded with a single classroom in Pune.

  2. 2014

    Launched women's skill-development centers across 5 states.

  3. 2017

    Reached 10,000 children through mobile learning labs.

  4. 2020

    Delivered 1M meals & health kits during the pandemic.

  5. 2023

    Planted 45,000 trees with 120 partner schools.

  6. 2026

    Now serving 126 villages and growing.

FAQ

Questions, answered.

Are donations tax exempt?+

Yes — HopeBridge is 80G certified. You will receive a tax-exempt receipt within 24 hours.

How is my money used?+

92% goes directly to program delivery. Annual audits are published on our Impact page.

Can I volunteer remotely?+

Absolutely. We have digital-tutoring, mentoring, design and translation roles that are fully remote.

Do you accept corporate CSR partnerships?+

Yes. We partner with 40+ corporates on CSR-compliant initiatives. Write to partnerships@hopebridge.org.

How do you measure impact?+

Every program has quarterly KPIs and independent third-party evaluations.
